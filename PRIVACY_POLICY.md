# Privacy Policy for Love Cafe

**Last Updated:** July 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Love Cafe ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to the Love Cafe mobile application and any related services.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the App, including:
* **Personal Data:** Name, email address, and phone number.
* **Location Data:** We may collect approximate location data to facilitate food delivery or locate the nearest cinema outlet.
* **Transaction Data:** History of your orders, purchases, and accumulated CinePoints.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
* To facilitate account creation and logon process.
* To fulfill and manage your food orders and payments.
* To manage your loyalty points (CinePoints).
* To respond to customer inquiries and offer support.
* To send administrative information to you.

## 4. How We Share Your Information
We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, to protect your rights, or to fulfill business obligations. We may share data with third-party vendors (like payment processors, e.g., Razorpay) who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf.

## 5. Security of Your Information
We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. All personal and financial data is encrypted in transit.

## 6. Your Rights
You may review, change, or terminate your account at any time. If you wish to delete your account or request data deletion, please contact us at support@lovecafe.org.in.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
**Email:** support@lovecafe.org.in
